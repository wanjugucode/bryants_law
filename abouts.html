About Us

Bryants Law LLP is a new and diverse team of highly skilled professionals providing outstanding legal services in an efficient, cost-effective and holistic manner.

We are all trained as lawyers but we recognize that not every client concern requires a traditional legal approach, instead we think outside of the box, working with our clients to identify and address all aspects of individual or organizational need, always striving to find strategic and practical yet comprehensive solutions.

Of course, if your matter needs to go to court, our team includes licensed advocates who do that too! ... While we are based in Nairobi, Kenya, our core team has a vast array of legal and non-legal experiences in other jurisdictions and, if our in-house experience does not address your need sufficiently,

we also have an ever-growing roster of local and international consultants that we call on as necessary to ensure that our clients get the most relevant and expert advice in a given situation.

We are committed to providing the highest quality client-centred legal services by applying a sound and ethical approach in a creative and dynamic team environment, with the ability to serve our clients in both English and Kiswahili.
