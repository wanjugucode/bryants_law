Biography

ADRIANNA A. McGARVEY

Adrianna, a UK citizen, a member of International Bar Association and resident of Kenya, with a distinguished teaching career spanning over 20 years and in two counties, and a First Class Honours Law Degree.

As a teacher, Adrianna decided to change career paths in 2014 and is now well on her way to becoming a practising advocate and distinguished scholar. Adrianna holds a B.Sc. (Hons) in Natural Resources and Management, a GRTTP - Post Graduate Diploma in Teaching Secondary Science, and has pursued an M.A. in Environmental Management (Policy & Law).

Adrianna obtained her First Class Honours degree from the University of Law in London in 2021 while managing a debt collection agency, and is pursuing an LL.M. in Law and Legal Practice Management from the same university. In 2021 she also obtained a Certificate in Arbitration and completed a course in OECD Anti-Bribery Recommendations from the International Bar Association, and is due to begin her Ph.D. in Law from the University of Buckingham in September 2022 while undertaking pupillage and continuing to manage a debt collection agency in Nairobi.

Adept at working under pressure and adapting to new situations and challenges, Adrianna passionately represents clients to realize positive solutions while minimizing risk. Her proven leadership and negotiation skills combined with strategic planning and business development expertise makes Adrianna an invaluable resource. When she is not studying or chasing after debtors, Adrianna enjoys weight training, riding horses

Areas of expertise: Education, environment, debt management
